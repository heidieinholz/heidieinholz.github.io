How to Make a PB&J Sandwich

  • Step One: Go to the pantry and retrieve peanut butter, jelly, and bread
  • Step Two: Place two slice of bread down on a cutting board.
  • Step Three: Open both jars. Use a butter knife to dip into the peanut butter, retrieving about 2-3 tablespoons.
  • Step Four: Apply the peanut butter to one slice of bread, spreading evenly until the bread is evenly coated with peanut butter. Wipe off remaining peanut butter onto the clean slice.
  • Step Five: Repeat Step Four, but substitute jelly for peanut butter.
  • Step Six: Place the peanut butter bread on top of the jelly bread, marrying together the peanut butter and jelly.
  • Step Seven: Cut the bread on a bias.
